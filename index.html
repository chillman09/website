How to connect

Follow these steps to link Studio to Spark:

1
Download the plugin from the Home tab and install it in Roblox Studio (Plugins → Manage Plugins → Install from file)
2
Open your game in Roblox Studio
3
Click the Spark plugin in your toolbar, paste your session token, and press Connect
4
Come back here and chat — changes will appear in Studio instantly
